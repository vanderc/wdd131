Movie Reviews

Iron Man 1

Rating: PG-13

A superhero movie about a billionaire inventor. He builds an armored suit to help him escape from people who have kidnapped him. He later improves his designs and creates multiple suits that he uses to fight crime.

Pirates of The Carribean

Rating: PG-13

As the movie starts we are introduced to Captain Jack Sparrow who is a captain without a ship and Elisabeth who is the Governor's daughter. She posses a coin that is linked to a legendary ship that has a crew who are cursed. We meet a blacksmith, Will Turner, who is a son of a pirate and the key to lifting the curse. The group of three go on an adventure in search of the ship the Black Pearl.

Jungle Cruise

Rating: PG-13

This movie is based on the ride Jungle Cruise at Disneyland. It is a good action adventure movie where we follow three characters as they search for a hidden tree that has a flower that posses a strong healing power. This flower can both heal illnesses and remove curses that are on people.

Clash of the Titans

Rating: PG-13

This is an action adventure movie. We follow the story of the demigod Perseus who is the son of Zeus. He is faced with the challenge to save a city from a war between man and Gods. He and a group of soldiers search for a way to fight back against the Gods who are using a krakin which is a big sea monster. Their search takes them all over including to the underworld where they find what they need to win their fight.

IF

Rating: PG

A children's movie about imaginary friends. A young girl discovers that she can see imaginary friends and embarks on an adventure to reconnect kids with forgotten imaginary friends.

Ghostbusters Frozen Empire

Rating: PG-13

A continuation of the new Ghostbusters. The Spenglers find an ancient artifact that releases an ancient evil. The new and old Ghostbusters unite in the fight agianst this ancient evil being.

Treasure Planet

Rating: PG

An adventure movie where we follow the teenager Jim Hawkins as he finds a map to a legendary treasure. He joins a ship that is full of bad pirates on his adventure to find the treasure.

My Spy

Rating: PG-13

A nine year old discovers that a CIA operative,JJ, is spying on her family. In exchange for the girl not blowing JJ's cover he teaches her how to become a spy. She later helps him with his mission.
