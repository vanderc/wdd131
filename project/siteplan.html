Overview

MovieReview

I enjoy watching movies and looking at others reviews on the movies that I enjoy. So I thought I would share my reviews.

Site Purpose

This website will let me share my reviews on movies that I have watched with others. It will have at least three separate pages one for images, one for movie descriptions, and one for a form page.

Scenarios

  • What are some good comedy movies?
  • What is a good action movie?
